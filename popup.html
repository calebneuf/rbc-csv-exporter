How to use
  1. Navigate to your RBC account transaction history
  2. Optional: Use the RBC date filter to set a date range (up to 7 years back for maximum history)
  3. The extension will automatically click "Show More" to load all transactions in the current view
  4. Click "Extract Transactions" to scan and collect all visible transactions
  5. Click "Download CSV" to save your transaction data
